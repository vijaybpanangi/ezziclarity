Books

Stories and guides
for finding your way.

Ezzi Clarity is beginning to write as well as advise. Our books carry the same belief as our consulting work: that everyone deserves a clear, kind path into something new. A student arriving at a Canadian university. A child arriving in a new country. Both deserve that.

Academic and career focused only. No immigration or visa advice provided.

Passion Project

A series for children making a new place home

Our heart-led work is a series of children's books about little ones from immigrant families learning a new language, a new school, new friends, while quietly holding onto the memories and small comforts of the home they left behind.

The stories are grounded in more than ten years of teaching children from early years through middle school. That means age-appropriate language, the right rhythm for each reader, and emotional honesty that actually reaches a child. These aren't books kids put down after one read.
