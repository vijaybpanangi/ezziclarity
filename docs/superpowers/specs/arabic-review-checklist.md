## Specific phrases flagged by the translators

### Home (`ar/index.html`)
1. **h1** "اكتشاف طريقك في مكان جديد." (for "Finding your footing in a new place"). Alternative: "إيجاد موطئ قدم في مكان جديد". Decide which reads better as a headline.
2. **Trust bar languages order** rendered "العربية · Français · English" (Arabic first) vs the EN order. Confirm the RTL-audience convention.
3. **Chapter labels** "الفصل الأول / الفصل الثاني" (Chapter one/two). Arabic doesn't always label page sections as "chapters"; consider "الجزء الأول/الثاني" or leaving them as eyebrow labels.

### Navigation (site-wide, all AR pages)
4. **"الاستشارات"** for the "Consulting" nav label. Standard term; confirm register for educational/career consulting.
5. **"الكتب"** for "Books" (carried from the Books page). Confirm.

### About (`ar/about/index.html`)
6. **h1** "نهج واضح ومنظّم لنجاح الطلاب في الحياة الأكاديمية." Dropped "experiential learning" from the headline; confirm or add "والتعلّم التجريبي".
7. **Realism principle** "نعمل مع الأشياء كما هي فعلاً" (We work with how things actually are). Alternative: "نتعامل مع الواقع كما هو".
8. **Gujarati spelling** "الغوجاراتية" vs "الغوجراتية" in the Language Capability section.

### Consulting (`ar/consulting/index.html`)
9. Page-header line "أخذ قرارات تشعر بالرضا عنها" (make choices you feel good about). Alternative: "اتخاذ قرارات مرضية".
10. Card 1 "بواقعية وطموح في آنٍ واحد" (realistic and ambitious at once). Confirm it reads naturally.

### Books (`ar/books/index.html`)
11. Intro split into three sentences ending "كلاهما يستحق ذلك." (Both deserve that). Confirm the rhythm/callback feels natural.

### Resources (`ar/resources/index.html`)
12. Section 2 heading "التعلّم التجريبي والتعاوني" (added "والتعاوني" to cover co-op). Confirm vs "التعلّم التجريبي" alone.
13. Card "تقييم بيئة العمل بموضوعية" for "Sizing Up a Workplace Honestly" — "بموضوعية" (objectively) vs "بصدق" (honestly).
14. Card "إجراء محادثات مهنية صادقة" for "Having Honest Career Conversations" — "صادقة" vs "واقعية".

### Contact (`ar/contact/index.html`)
15. Eyebrow "يسعدنا أن نسمع منك" (We'd love to hear from you). Confirm warmth/brand voice.
16. "إن كان سؤالك لا يزال في طور التشكّل" (If your question is still taking shape) — somewhat literal; a more idiomatic phrasing may be preferred.
